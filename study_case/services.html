Managerial Procurement

Managerial Procurement service involves several tasks such as:

  • Submitting requests for procurement,
  • Managing bids and offers,
  • Coordinating procurement processes, and
  • Giving approval for procurement needed for the project.
🖥

Vendor Management

Vendor Management service focuses on tasks for vendors, such as:

  • Accepting requests for quotation (RFQ),
  • Sending bids, and
  • Following the status of their bids.
✓

Project Management

Project Management service is responsible for overseeing project requirements, such as:

  • Ensuring that procurement is conducted according to the project needs,
  • Coordinating with the vendor to fulfill project requirements, and
  • Evaluating and closing the procurement process.
